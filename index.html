Our Research Divisions

Science That Changes Everything

Three pioneering divisions working in concert to deliver the next generation of human medicine.

Virology

Umbrella Corporation's virology division stands at the forefront of infectious disease research. Our scientists work tirelessly to identify, isolate, and neutralise emerging viral threats before they reach the global population. Through proprietary compound synthesis and advanced pathogen mapping, we develop antiviral therapies that save millions of lives each year.

Genetic Research

Our genetic research programme represents decades of pioneering work in the field of molecular biology. Utilising cutting-edge gene therapy platforms and next-generation sequencing technology, Umbrella's geneticists are rewriting the possibilities of human medicine.

Oncology

Cancer remains one of humanity's greatest challenges — and Umbrella Corporation is committed to meeting it head on. Our oncology division develops targeted biologic therapies and immunotherapy programmes designed to disrupt tumour growth at a cellular level.
