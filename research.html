Virology

Viral Research

Umbrella Corporation's virology division stands at the forefront of disease research. Our scientists work tirelessly to identify, isolate, and neutralise emerging viral threats before they reach the global population.

Through proprietary compound synthesis and advanced pathogen mapping, we develop antiviral therapies that save millions of lives each year. Our T-series antigen research program has produced over 40 clinically approved compounds since 1991.

Genetics

Genetic Engineering

Our genetics division pioneers the future of human potential. We specialise in gene editing, synthetic biology, and regenerative medicine to enhance human capabilities and treat genetic disorders.

From our groundbreaking CRISPR-based therapies to our work on human augmentation, we are committed to unlocking the full potential of the human genome. Our research has led to over 30 patented technologies and 15 approved treatments for previously incurable conditions.

15 Approved Treatments

Oncology

Cancer Research

Our oncology division is dedicated to conquering cancer through innovative research and cutting-edge therapies. We focus on immunotherapy, targeted drug development, and early detection technologies to improve patient outcomes.

With over 25 patented treatments and a robust pipeline of novel therapies, we are making significant strides in the fight against cancer. Our collaborations with leading medical institutions worldwide ensure that our research translates into real-world impact.
